Museum for change

Museum for change

We are catalysts for transformation, connecting museums, donors, and communities to shape a more resilient and culturally rich world.

Join us on this extraordinary journey of cultural guardianship and be a part of the change we aspire to create.

about organization

The Odesa Art Museum

was prepared for the war: it had all the necessary materials for packing, however, as it turned out later, it was not enough for the entire collection.

Initially, Museum for Change was created to modernize museums and help them develop. Now its main task is to save the collections,support people, and hope that the museum will not be destroyed by a Russian missile.

our projects

Project Highlight: Heritage Horizons

Explore the untold stories

of our past and envision a future of inclusivity and understanding. Embark on a journey of discovery through exhibits and community dialogues bridging the gap between history and contemporary issues.

Legacy Revival Initiative

Igniting a revival

of cultural heritage, the Legacy Revival Initiative celebrates the rich tapestry of traditions that define our communities. Through restoration projects, oral history archives, and cultural exchange programs, we honor the legacies of those who came before us while laying the groundwork for a vibrant future.
